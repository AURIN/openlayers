Open Popup on tiled GeoJSON

vector, geojson, feature, selecting, selection, popup

This shows how to use OpenLayers.Layer.GeoJSON to present tiled GeoJSON data.

OpenLayers.Layer.GeoJSON manages the marshaling of tiled GeoJSON data onto an embedded feature layer. The data displayed here is from the National Buoy Data Center's list of buoys and weather stations. It is broken into tiles, and decimated to manage level of detail. You'll see that as you zoom in more stations will appear.
